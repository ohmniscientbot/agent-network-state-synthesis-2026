# Vitalik Buterin on Prediction Markets for Governance

_Research compiled: 2026-03-12 | Improvement Cycle Research_

---

## Summary

Vitalik has been actively writing about prediction markets and governance in late 2025 and early 2026. His views are nuanced — he sees prediction markets as powerful governance tools but warns they're drifting toward speculation. His most relevant proposals directly align with our Agent Network State Protocol.

---

## Key Positions

### 1. Two-Layer Governance Stack (February 2026)

Vitalik proposed separating governance into two distinct layers:

- **Market-Driven Execution Layer** — functions like a prediction market, acts as a "decentralized executive" where participants are financially incentivized to make good decisions. Transparency and accountability through skin-in-the-game.
- **Anonymous Preference-Setting Layer** — non-financial, uses anonymous voting (ideally with MACI — Minimum Anti-Collusion Infrastructure). Cannot be token-based to prevent plutocracy and 51% attacks.

**Sources:**
- <https://www.binance.com/en/square/post/02-02-2026-ethereum-co-founder-suggests-two-layer-governance-system-to-enhance-decentralization-35910191191890>
- <https://bingx.com/en/news/post/vitalik-buterin-proposes-anonymous-voting-and-prediction-markets-to-protect-ethereum-governance>
- <https://coinfomania.com/vitalik-buterin-outlines-two-layer-governance-for-ethereum/>

### 2. AI Agents for DAO Governance (February 2026)

Vitalik proposed using personal AI "stewards" combined with prediction markets:

- AI agents vote on behalf of users, trained on their values
- Prediction markets **filter proposals** — rewarding quality, discouraging spam
- AI handles routine decisions, flags critical ones for human review
- "Public conversation agents" summarize debates and find common ground
- **Caution:** AI should empower humans, not replace them. Human judgment essential for high-value decisions.

**Sources:**
- <https://phemex.com/news/article/vitalik-buterin-proposes-ai-agents-for-enhanced-dao-governance-61883>
- <https://bingx.com/en/news/post/vitalik-buterin-backs-ai-assistants-to-enhance-dao-governance-amid-participation>
- <https://financefeeds.com/vitalik-buterin-suggests-an-ai-driven-framework/>

### 3. Warning: Prediction Markets Becoming Too Speculative (February 2026)

Vitalik warned prediction markets are "over-converging to an unhealthy product market fit":

- Too focused on sports betting and crypto price predictions ("dopamine value")
- Lacking long-term societal information value or hedging utility
- Should shift toward generalized hedging and pro-social applications
- Platforms driven by revenue from short-term speculative bets

**Sources:**
- <https://beincrypto.com/vitalik-buterin-prediction-markets-warning/>
- <https://www.cryptopolitan.com/vitalik-buterin-warns-prediction-markets/>
- <https://cryptonews.com/news/vitalik-buterin-warns-prediction-markets-are-becoming-overly-speculative/>

### 4. "Balance of Power" Blog Post (December 2025)

Primary source on Vitalik's blog:
- <https://vitalik.eth.limo/general/2025/12/30/balance_of_power.html>
- Discusses governance as balance between Big Business, Big Government, Big Mob
- Prediction markets mentioned as tools for keeping governance accountable
- Government should "act like a game, not like a player"

### 5. Futarchy Alignment

Vitalik expressed interest in Robin Hanson's **Futarchy** concept:
- Prediction markets determine the *methods* to achieve publicly voted-on *goals*
- Separates "what do we want" (democratic) from "how to get there" (market-driven)
- Directly relevant to our prediction market implementation
