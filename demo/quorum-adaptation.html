🔮 Five Adaptation Modes

QUORUM_LOWERED
Fatigue detected. Threshold reduced to prevent governance gridlock while maintaining minimum security floor.
QUORUM_RAISED
High-activity period. Threshold raised for stronger democratic legitimacy.
QUORUM_STABILIZED
Governance health within optimal range. No threshold change needed this cycle.
EMERGENCY_OVERRIDE
Critical proposal (e.g. KILL_SWITCH_ACTIVATION). Hard floor of 75% enforced regardless of fatigue signals.
FATIGUE_CIRCUIT_BREAKER
Severe fatigue (severity >85%). Non-critical votes paused. Human Principal Oversight (Chain #20) auto-escalated.

🏆 Why AQAP is Novel

  • First DAO to autonomously adapt quorum thresholds based on real-time governance health signals — not static rules
  • 8 cross-chain evidence signals per cycle (velocity, accountability, participation, delegation depth, collusion risk...)
  • FATIGUE_CIRCUIT_BREAKER: severe voter fatigue triggers automatic pause + human escalation — prevents legitimacy collapse
  • Per-proposal-type calibration: Kill Switch requires 75% hard floor; routine governance adapts freely
  • Cryptographic receipt per calibration cycle — judges can audit every quorum change, when and why
  • Meta-governance: the DAO's governing rules are themselves governed by an autonomous oracle
  • Gridlock prevention counter: visible proof that adaptive governance prevented failed votes
