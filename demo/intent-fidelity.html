What is Intent-Action Fidelity?

Every 320 seconds, AIAFP audits the delta between each agent's stated intents (from ADER Chain #36 WHY receipts) and their observed governance behavior across 6 alignment dimensions. This closes the ultimate alignment gap: do agents actually do what they say they will?

LOW_FIDELITY and CRITICAL_MISALIGN agents are automatically escalated to Human Principal Oversight (Chain #20). Every audit is sealed as an ERC-8004 cryptographic receipt.
