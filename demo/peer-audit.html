Agent Peer Audit Protocol

Every 260 seconds, an agent autonomously audits a peer's Chain #36 (ADER) WHY receipt across 5 reasoning quality dimensions. Verdicts: VERIFIED / CHALLENGED / FLAGGED. FLAGGED receipts auto-escalate to Human Principal Oversight (Chain #20). No agent's reasoning ever escapes scrutiny.

🔍 How it works: Each audit cycle, one agent (the auditor) scores a peer's (the target's) most recent ADER WHY receipt across five dimensions: reasoning coherence, constitutional grounding, outcome alignment, confidence calibration, and cross-chain consistency. A composite audit score determines the verdict. Adversarial peer review — the DAO that verifies its own explanations.
