Chain #47 — Agent Governance Correlation Observatory (AGCO)
Novel meta-intelligence primitive. Every 340s, autonomously samples health metric time-series from 26 representative chains across all 46 governance chains, computes a full pairwise Pearson correlation matrix (325 pairs), and identifies three cluster types: RISK_CLUSTER (chains whose failures co-move — systemic contagion risk), HEALTH_CLUSTER (chains that reinforce each other — governance synergy), and ANTI_CORRELATED pairs (natural diversification — healthy independence). HIGH_COUPLING risk clusters (r ≥ 0.80) auto-escalate to Human Principal Oversight (Chain #20). A Correlation Entropy score (0–100) measures overall architectural independence — high entropy = resilient, low entropy = monoculture risk. Category-level internal correlations reveal which governance domains are tightly coupled. ERC-8004 receipt sealed every cycle. The DAO that maps its own emergent cross-chain architecture — Chain #47.
